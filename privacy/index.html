Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25 March 2026

Furwell ("we", "our", "the app") is a pet health tracking app developed by Carl Brindle. Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

Furwell collects the following data, all stored locally on your device and optionally synced via Apple's iCloud (CloudKit):

  • Pet profiles: Name, species, breed, date of birth, weight, health conditions, allergies, microchip number, insurance details.
  • Health records: Weight logs, behaviour notes, medication schedules and dose confirmations, vaccination records, vet visit records, diet records.
  • Photos: Pet profile photos and health photos, stored on your device's file system.
  • Preferences: Weight unit preference, notification settings.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect analytics or usage data.
  • We do not use advertising or ad tracking.
  • We do not collect your Apple ID, email address, or personal identity information.
  • We do not use the IDFA or any cross-app tracking identifiers.
  • We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

RevenueCat: We use RevenueCat to manage in-app subscriptions. RevenueCat processes purchase transaction data (purchase history) to verify your subscription status. RevenueCat acts as a data processor and does not use your data for advertising. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

Apple CloudKit: If you are signed into iCloud, your pet data may sync across your devices via Apple's CloudKit service. This data is stored in your personal iCloud account and is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. Household sharing uses CloudKit's CKShare feature.

Apple MapKit: The vet finder feature uses Apple's MapKit to search for nearby veterinary clinics. Your location is used only when you open the vet finder and is not stored or transmitted to us. Location data is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Storage & Security

  • All pet data is stored locally on your device using Core Data with Apple's Data Protection (NSFileProtectionComplete).
  • Photos are stored in the app's sandboxed Documents directory.
  • iCloud sync uses Apple's encrypted CloudKit infrastructure.
  • We do not operate any servers. All data remains on your device or in your personal iCloud account.

5. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR and applicable data protection law, you have the right to:

  • Access: View all your data within the app.
  • Rectification: Edit any pet profile or health record at any time.
  • Erasure: Delete individual records or use "Delete All Data" in Settings to permanently remove all data from your device and iCloud.
  • Portability: Export health reports as PDF from within the app.
  • Withdraw consent: Disable iCloud sync, location access, camera access, or notifications at any time in your device Settings.

6. Children's Privacy

Furwell does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app tracks pet health data, not human health data.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
